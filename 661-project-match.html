How it works

The goal is simple: help homeowners avoid chasing contractors while helping serious local pros receive cleaner project requests.

Step 01

Upload photos

Show the room, repair, exterior area, pool, fence, flooring, landscaping, or project space clearly.

Step 02

Describe the goal

Tell us what you want fixed, upgraded, repaired, or priced, including your timeline and city.

Step 03

Get connected

Your project request will be reviewed and routed to a preferred independent local pro based on your project type, service area, and availability. Your first name, phone number, city, photos, and project details will be shared so the pro can contact you directly about your request.

Good • Better • Best project planning

When possible, projects are organized into simple planning ranges so homeowners can compare practical, balanced, and premium approaches before wasting time on vague calls.

Good

Budget-conscious

Focused on solving the core issue with a practical lower-cost approach.

Better

Balanced value

Better materials, cleaner finish work, and a more complete scope.

Best

Premium finish

Higher-end materials, larger upgrades, and more complete transformation work.

Important: Any pricing discussed before a site visit is informational only. Final pricing, contracts, permits, insurance, warranties, licensing, and job responsibility are handled directly between the homeowner and the independent contractor.

Preferred pro standards

661 Project Match is designed around responsiveness, professionalism, and homeowner experience.

Minimum Standards
  • Active license where required
  • Google Business Profile
  • Strong review history
  • Proof of insurance when applicable
  • Professional communication
Response Expectations
  • Fast homeowner acknowledgment during business hours
  • Goal of contractor contact within 30–60 minutes whenever possible
  • Repeated missed follow-ups may affect preferred status
  • Homeowner experience matters more than lowest pricing

Preferred Pro Rotation

Two preferred pros per category to start.

The goal is not to flood dozens of contractors with the same lead. Most categories start with two preferred pros per service area. A third backup pro may be added when demand is high or when extra coverage is needed.
